8. Edit & View Menus Operations

From the edit menu you can undo and redo your last operations. You can also cut, copy, and paste a thing (after selecting it). These operations include cutting/copying/pasting the thing properties and not only its graphical symbol.

From the view menu you can make graphical zooming in and out of the current OPD, and refresh the screen view.
