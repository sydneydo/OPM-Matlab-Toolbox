4. Structural Relations 

    4.3 Generalization - Specialization

Generalization - Specialization relations extend the inheritance concept to both objects and processes. 

The symbol of Generalization - Specialization relations is a white triangle.


Pot, Pan, and Gril are Cooking Tools.
Boiling, Frying, and Grilling are Cooking.

Meaning: Pot, Pan, and Gril have at least the same structural relations and procedural relations as Cooking tool. 
Boiling, Frying, and Grilling have at least the same structural relations and procedural relations as Cooking.

The object and processes in this case are connected via instrument links.
