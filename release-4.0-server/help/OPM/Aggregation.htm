4. Structural Relations 

    4.2 Aggregation - Participation

Aggregation - Participation relations inform us, that one of two connected things (objects or processes) is actually the part of the of other  one. 

Aggregation - Participation relations are denoted by black triangles. 


Consortium consists of 5 Banks.

Meaning: five  Banks are the parts of the whole Consortium.
