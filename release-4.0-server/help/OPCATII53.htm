5. OPM Notation Operations 

    5.3 Add Structural Relation

In order to connect two things with a structural relation you should add the things first, and click the required relation symbol. Then click the source thing and drag it towards the destination thing. Afterwards you can double click the relation in order to specify the cardinality. You can choose between 1, many, and custom (where you should enter a value, e.g. 2..5, 0..1, etc.). In the Misc tab of the relation properties you can specify the relation border, fill, and text colors.

For the unidirection and bidirectional relations, you can specify in addition the source and destination cardinality and the relation name (Meaning for unidirectional relations, and Forward/Backward Meaning for bidirectional relations). You can arrange the general relations manually by first clicking the right button of the mouse, then unchecking the autoarrange property, and draging the relevant edge. The edge can be dragged to another object or process (if OPM rules allow it). In this case, the new object/process become the new source/destination for the relation.
