6. Scaling Operations 

Choose the object or process which you want to detail and then choose the unfolding or in-zooming possibility from the menu bar (Scaling) or from the mouse right button menu.

For unfolding you will be asked if you want the software to bring all the things you have already connected to the unfolded thing (in the project). Then you can continue modeling the system in the separate unfolded OPD.

For in-zooming, the software brings all the links associated with the in-zoomed thing. You can continue specifying the in-zoomed thing by adding inner objects and processes and by changing the source/destination of the procedural links.

Pay attention: Since OPCAT II development is in progress, not all the scaling rules are applied.
