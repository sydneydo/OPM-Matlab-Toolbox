5. OPM Notation Operations 

    5.7 Change Object/Process/State/Relation/Link Properties

When you need to change object, process, state, relation, or link properties you can double click them or click the right button of the mouse. In general, clicking the right button of the mouse opens a menu of relevant operations and differs according to the entity on which you click.
